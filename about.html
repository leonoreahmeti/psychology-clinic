About Me

Professional care with a personal approach

Nafie Sylejmani

I am a licensed clinical psychologist (MSc, University of Tirana) and registered with the Order of Nurses and Midwives of Kosovo (OIK). I provide online therapy sessions to support individuals in managing stress, anxiety, and emotional challenges from the comfort of their own home.

My approach is personalized, compassionate, and evidence-based, designed to meet each client’s unique needs. I work with children, adults, parents, and marginalized groups, offering guidance, emotional support, and professional development programs.

Experience

Over 5 years of professional experience in therapy and counseling, including working with diverse populations and designing mental health programs for individuals and organizations.

Certifications

  • Certified Clinical Psychologist
  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
  • Mental Health Counseling

Why Choose Online Sessions?

Online sessions provide the flexibility to schedule therapy at a time that works for you, without needing to travel. Even a single online session can help you clarify your goals and begin meaningful progress toward emotional well-being.
